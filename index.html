How it works

Three steps.
One minute.

No setup. No meditation streak to protect. Just open the app, tell it what you're feeling, and follow along.

01
01

Name what's heavy

Choose from five honest states — overwhelmed, anxious, distracted, overthinking, or restless. No wrong answer. Just what's closest to how you feel right now.

⟶ Takes 2 seconds
02
02

Follow the breath

Each state has its own science-backed pattern. Box breathing for overwhelm. 4-7-8 for anxiety. A moving dot for when your mind won't focus. Built for that feeling, not a generic calm.

⟶ 60 seconds minimum
03
03

Come back to yourself

One minute shifts your nervous system. Sessions are logged automatically. Over time, Reflect shows you your patterns — when stress peaks, what states you visit most.

⟶ Tracked automatically
